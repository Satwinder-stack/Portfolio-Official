StandSpeakAct Blog

PURPOSE

StandSpeakAct is a dynamic blogging platform designed to amplify voices on current global trends and social issues. The project focuses on high-readability typography and a robust content management structure, providing a space for meaningful discourse and real-time updates on world events.

CORE FEATURES

• Dynamic content feed categorized by trending topics and urgency.
• Specialized UI experience for more dynamic experience.
• SEO-optimized article pages with rich media integration.
• Filtering of articles based on the user's interests.
